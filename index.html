1. Why Mexico ?✈️

Mexico is a magical and beautiful country. With its historical monuments, its dreamy landscapes and its paradisiacal beaches, Mexico is THE country to go to! Located between the United States and Central America, Mexico offers a wide variety of landscapes: mountains, deserts, jungles...

2. What is Mexico ?

Mexico is a vibrant country known for its rich culture, colorful traditions, and delicious cuisine. From ancient Mayan and Aztec ruins to lively cities like Mexico City, it offers a unique mix of history and modern life. Visitors can explore beautiful beaches, deserts, and jungles across its diverse landscapes. Mexico is also famous for its warm hospitality, music, and festivals.
